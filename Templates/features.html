Key Features of PHASAL

🌿 AI-Powered Disease Detection

Upload a plant leaf image and our AI model will identify the disease in seconds with high accuracy using deep learning and image processing techniques.

📸 Real-Time Image Analysis

No delays or waiting — PHASAL gives instant results by processing uploaded images on the fly with a smart backend engine.

💡 Detailed Disease Insights

Along with the disease name, you'll receive information on the cause and suggested treatment or prevention methods for sustainable farming.

🗣️ Multilingual Support (Coming Soon)

We're working on supporting regional Indian languages like Hindi, Marathi, and Tamil so farmers can use PHASAL comfortably in their mother tongue.

🧪 Constant Model Training

Our system keeps learning from more images and data, improving the accuracy and adapting to new diseases and crop types.

📊 Data-Driven Agriculture

Collected disease data can help in mapping outbreaks and guiding agricultural authorities for better farming policies and disease control.
